I am an aspiring engineer . I am the creator of this website Agriniser . Agriniser connects farmers directly to customers .

Objective & Goal

Since childhood i have seen the farmers of my village struggling to get the right price for their products.This inspired me to create a platform through which farmers can sell their products globally directly to the customers at a price that is best suited for their products.This eliminates the role retailers and middlemen.This enables the customers to buy products at a much lower price directly from the farmers.
